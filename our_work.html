Our Work

What originated as a scholarship initiative has blossomed into a comprehensive approach to the development of children. Beyond financial support, the trust now offers mentorship, medical camps, extracurricular activities, and academic sessions.

Over the years, Sarva Sewa Trust has made a significant impact, aiding over 10,000 children in their educational pursuits.




Acadamic Scholarships


Sarva Sewa Trust, since its inception in 1985, has been dedicated to providing scholarships to deserving children. What began as a core initiative has expanded over the years, supporting students financially to pursue their educational goals. The trust recognizes the pivotal role that financial assistance plays in unlocking the potential of bright young minds.



Eye Camps


In an effort to enhance healthcare accessibility, Sarva Sewa Trust organizes Eye camps. Prescription eyewear, including powered glasses, is offered free of cost. The trust's focus on eye care contributes to the overall well-being of the community it serves. Further, cataract operations for older family members are provided at heavily subsidised rates.

Dental Camps


Sarva Sewa Trust extends its healthcare initiatives to dental camps, providing essential dental care services to students and their families.The trust ensures that dental care is provided free of cost, underscoring its commitment to the overall health of the community.



Acadamic Lessons


The commitment to education at Sarva Sewa Trust extends to providing academic lessons and teaching sessions. Beyond traditional scholarship programs, the organization ensures that students receive quality education through focused lessons, contributing to their overall academic excellence.
Currently, students and teachers from all over the country are working with us to share their knowledge and help young learners. Volunteer today to support students in need.

Sport Camps


Recognizing the importance of a holistic development approach, Sarva Sewa Trust encourages sports participation among the students. Sports activities not only promote physical fitness but also instill qualities of teamwork, discipline, and perseverance, contributing to the well-rounded growth of the beneficiaries.



Extra-Curricular Activities


Extracurricular activities are an integral part of Sarva Sewa Trust's approach to child development. From cultural events to skill-building workshops, students are provided with opportunities to explore their talents and interests beyond the academic realm, fostering a diverse range of skills and passions.



Counselling Sessions


Sarva Sewa Trust places a strong emphasis on mental health. Professional counselling services are readily available to address teenage issues, mental health concerns, and depression. This proactive approach ensures that the emotional well-being of the students is prioritized alongside their academic journey. They are also taught to be kind, empathetic and encouraged to give back to society in whatever capacity they can.
